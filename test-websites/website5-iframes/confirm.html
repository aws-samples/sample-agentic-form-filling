Terms of Carriage

1. Check-in Requirements

Passengers must check in at least 2 hours before departure for domestic flights. Late arrivals may be denied boarding without refund.

2. Baggage Policy

AnyCompany Iframes operates with an unbundled fare structure. Only one small personal item (40x30x15cm) is included. All other baggage incurs additional fees.

3. Seat Assignment

Seats are subject to availability and may be changed at the airline's discretion for operational reasons.

4. No-Show Policy

Passengers who fail to board their flight forfeit all rights to refund or rebooking.

5. Flight Changes

AnyCompany Iframes reserves the right to change flight times with notice. Significant changes entitle passengers to rebooking or refund.
